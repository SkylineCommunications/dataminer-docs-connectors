# Blankom DRD-700

## About

The Blankom DRD-700 is a versatile and high-performance video distribution solution designed for professional broadcast environments. The connector provides a unified view of the DRD-700 Quad Multistream Processor, enabling fast monitoring and easy configuration.

## Key Features

- **Reliable 24/7 monitoring**: Continuous supervision of all inputs, outputs, CAM modules, and transport streams to ensure stable operation.

- **Unified configuration**: Configure ASI routing, IP input/output, DVB frontends, and the device itself from a single, clean interface.

- **Transport stream insights**: Get a clear overview of all incoming and outgoing transport streams, along with their respective sources and destinations, such as DVB, ASI, or IP-based.
