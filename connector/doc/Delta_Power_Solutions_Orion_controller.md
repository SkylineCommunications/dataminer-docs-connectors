# Delta Power Orion Controller
 
## About
 
The **Delta Power Orion Controller** is an advanced DC power management system designed for monitoring and controlling critical power infrastructure, including rectifiers, battery strings, and power distribution units (PDUs). It provides real-time visibility into key electrical parameters such as voltage, current, load distribution, and battery charging behaviour.
 
The system is engineered for telecom and industrial power environments where high availability, efficiency, and reliability are essential. It supports configuration of charging modes, protection thresholds, and distribution settings to ensure optimal performance and extended battery life.
 
With structured views for system inventory, load monitoring, battery management, and PDU configuration, the controller enables efficient operation, maintenance, and troubleshooting of DC power systems.
 
---
 
## Key Features
 
- **Comprehensive DC power monitoring**: Real-time visibility of rectifiers, batteries, and PDU channels  

- **Battery management support**: Monitor and control charge modes such as float, boost, and equalize  

- **Configurable protection settings**: Define voltage thresholds, LVD behavior, and alarm suppression  

- **Detailed PDU control and monitoring**: Manage channel configuration and real-time load measurements  

- **System inventory overview**: View installed hardware components and system topology  

- **Flexible parameter configuration**: Adapt settings per operational and site requirements  

- **Alarm and threshold monitoring**: Detect abnormal conditions and ensure system stability
