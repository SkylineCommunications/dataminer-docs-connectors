# Ciena Navigator

## About

Ciena Navigator is a DataMiner connector that integrates with Ciena platforms to monitor health, status, and performance via HTTP-based APIs. It focuses on visibility and operational assurance for Ciena infrastructure in a unified DataMiner environment.

## Key Features

- **Unified monitoring**: Centralized visibility for Ciena devices and services.
- **Status and alarms**: Retrieves device status and exposes actionable alarms.
- **Performance insights**: Polls performance metrics suitable for dashboards and reporting.
- **Redundancy support**: Optional secondary connection configuration for high availability.
- **Extensible workflows**: Compatible with DataMiner dashboards, reports, and correlations.

## Use Cases

### Network Operations Visibility

**Challenge**: Limited visibility across distributed Ciena infrastructure.

**Solution**: Consolidated device health and KPIs in DataMiner dashboards.

**Benefit**: Faster fault isolation and improved SLA adherence.

### Proactive Assurance

**Challenge**: Detecting degradations before they escalate.

**Solution**: Thresholds and alarms driven by polled metrics.

**Benefit**: Reduced MTTR and fewer service disruptions.
