# Grass Valley UHD1200

## About

The Grass Valley UHD1200 connector enables comprehensive monitoring and control of the Grass Valley KudosPro UHD1200 video conversion platform. Designed for professional broadcast environments, it provides centralized management of UHD, HD, and SD conversion workflows, allowing operators to efficiently configure video processing, audio routing, timing, and metadata handling from within DataMiner.

## Key Features

- **Comprehensive video processing control**: Configure and monitor video conversion settings including input/output formats, aspect ratio control, frame rate conversion, color processing, and film cadence optimization.
- **Integrated audio management**: Control audio routing, AES and analog interfaces, audio shuffling, and per-channel audio processing from a single interface.
- **Advanced synchronization and metadata support**: Configure Genlock, timecode, and metadata processing to ensure accurate synchronization and reliable transport of broadcast services.
