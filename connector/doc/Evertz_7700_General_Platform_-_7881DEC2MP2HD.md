# Evertz 7700 General Platform - 7881DEC2MP2HD

> [!NOTE]
> This connector is deprecated. Please use [Evertz 7x00 General Platform](xref:Connector_help_Evertz_7x00_General_Platform) instead.

## About

The **7881DEC2MP2HD** card is a high-quality SD/HD MPEG2 decoder card. It offers high-end SD and HD MPEG-2 decoding of encoded streams. It is perfect for monitoring applications or decoding for downstream baseband video and audio processing. It can reconstruct in the most flexible way all VANC and HANC data in the HD/SDI output and can be coupled with Evertz RF frontend (7780DM2-LB DVB-S2 demodulator) and Evertz downstream video and audio processing equipment to create a complete versatile integrated receiver/decoder.

An **SNMP** connection is used in order to successfully retrieve and configure the device's information. There are different possibilities for **alarming monitoring** and **trending**.

This connector is automatically generated by connector **Evertz 7700 General Platform**.
