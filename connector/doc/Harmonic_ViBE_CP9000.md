# Harmonic ViBE CP9000

## About

This is a DataMiner connector for the **Harmonic RD9000 Decoder**, a contribution encoder that preserves video quality at the front of the broadcast chain by processing uncompressed UHD signals.

### Key Features

- **Alarm monitoring of the devices**: Every alarm triggered from the devices can be monitored in real time.
- **Configuration**: Configure the encoder settings to satisfy operational requirements.
