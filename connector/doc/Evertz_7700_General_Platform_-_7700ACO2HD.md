# Evertz 7700 General Platform - 7700ACO2HD

> [!NOTE]
> This connector is deprecated. Please use [Evertz 7x00 General Platform](xref:Connector_help_Evertz_7x00_General_Platform) instead.

## About

The **Evertz 7700ACO2HD** card is a smart automatic changeover (ACO) for high definition 1.5 Gb/s or standard definition 270 Mb/s serial digital video signals.

This device provides:

- Dual processing paths on a single module.
- Smart HD/SD-SDI automatic protection switching of both paths.
- Video and audio proc amp control.
- Built-in trouble slide per ACO.
- Optional IntelliGain audio loudness processing with dynamic audio range control.

An **SNMP** connection is used in order to successfully retrieve and configure the device's information. There are different possibilities for **alarming monitoring** and **trending**.

This connector is automatically generated by connector **Evertz 7700 General Platform**.
