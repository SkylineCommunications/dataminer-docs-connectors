# Zaptec Platform

## About

The Zaptec Platform connector enables centralized monitoring and management of Zaptec EV charging infrastructure. It integrates with the Zaptec API to give operators full visibility over installations, chargers, and charging sessions across their entire charging network.

## Key Features

- **Installation and charger monitoring**: Track the status and operational state of all chargers and installations within the Zaptec platform from a single interface.
- **Charging session visibility**: Monitor active and completed charging sessions, including energy consumption, session timing, and user information.
